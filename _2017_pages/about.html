Theme

Our theme, and what we're really about, is everyone having a good time and learning. That's why we put the effort in and work to make sure this event comes at no cost to any of our participants.

Mission

We are a not-for-profit event team working to provide students with an opportunity to dive deeper into the field of aerospace and mechatronics engineering through this hands-on team experience. In Canada, there are few opportunities to explore RC aviation and RC in general, and we want to be the ones who break this obstacle while having a lot of fun and helping participants develop essential engineering skills.

Vision

Overall, we envision bringing people together and get those thinking muscles running outside of your typical school setting, and we hope to push these experiences into a future in engineering.
